# Submissions

This is where you hand in your work. There is **one folder for each week**.

Each week has **3 lessons**. After you finish a week, you submit your **3 completed
`practice.ipynb` notebooks** (the ones where you did the assignment at the bottom).

---

## How to submit (4 steps)

1. Go to the folder for the week, for example `submissions/week-1/`.
2. Make a folder with **your own name**, like `submissions/week-1/aziz-karimov/`.
   - Use small letters. Use a dash `-` instead of a space. No Uzbek letters (ў, қ, ғ, ҳ).
3. Put your **3 finished notebooks** inside your folder:
   - `lesson-1.ipynb`, `lesson-2.ipynb`, `lesson-3.ipynb` (use the right lesson numbers for that week).
4. Save / upload it to GitHub (your mentor will show you how — usually a **Pull Request**).

> Look at the folder `_TEMPLATE/` to see exactly how your folder should look.

---

## What counts as "finished"

A finished notebook means:
- **Every cell runs** with no error (Runtime → Run all).
- You did the **assignment** at the bottom: comments in your own words + short answers.
- Your **name** is written in the first cell.

## Rules

- Submit **only your own work**. Copying another student's notebook is not allowed.
- One folder per student, per week. Do not touch another student's folder.
- If you are stuck, ask your mentor **before** the deadline, not after.
